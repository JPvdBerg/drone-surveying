Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. This policy explains how JDHoffman Aerial Solutions ("we", "us") handles personal information, in line with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

Who we are

JDHoffman Aerial Solutions, a drone surveying and inspection business operating across greater Gauteng. Responsible party contact: divviej1@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Enquiry details you submit through our contact form: your name, email address, phone number, company (optional), the service you're interested in, and your message.
  • Anonymous usage data: we log basic, cookieless events (for example, a page view or a tap on the call/WhatsApp/quote buttons) to understand how the site is used. This does not identify you and is not linked to your enquiry.

Why we collect it

Enquiry details are used solely to respond to your request and provide a quote. Usage data is used only to improve the website. We do not sell your information or use it for unrelated marketing.

How it's stored

Form submissions and anonymous events are stored using Google Firebase (Firestore), which acts as our data processor, on secured Google Cloud infrastructure. Access is restricted to us.

Retention

We keep enquiry details only as long as needed to handle your request and for reasonable follow-up, after which they are deleted on request or when no longer required.

Your rights (POPIA)

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing divviej1@gmail.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

Changes

We may update this policy; the latest version will always be available at this page.
